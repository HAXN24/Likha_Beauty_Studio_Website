The Likha Story

Inspired by 'Lik-ha,' the Filipino term for 'creation,' we bring the essence of artistic creation to each nail service we provide. Discover how Likha Beauty Studio blossomed from simple roots into a cherished part of the Prince George community. Our timeline highlights the milestones and innovations that define us. Step into our history to understand our unwavering commitment to artistry and elegance in nail care.

Our Humble Beginnings

May 2022

With paintbrushes in hand and our loyal dog Coco by our side, we transformed a blank space into Likha Beauty Studio.

From Canvas to Masterpiece

July 2022

From bare walls to a haven of elegance, Likha Beauty Studio found its form. Our walls came alive with abstract drawings, hand-painted by us! Our space had become a living testament to our artistic vision, now fully equipped and inviting, ready to craft beauty in every detail.

Where Comfort Meets Craftsmanship

August 2022

The moment our chairs and pedicure stations were assembled, it was as if the last pieces of a beautiful puzzle fell into place.

Graced for Beauty

August 2022

Before officially opening our doors, we invited Fr. Mike to bless the salon. It was a solemn moment that imbued our space with a sense of grace and purpose, preparing us to bring beauty and joy to everyone who enters.

Dreamed by Two, Crafted for You

August 2022

Maria and Yan stood together, gazing at the salon they had brought to life. Their shared dream had become a reality, a space where artistry and community would flourish. Their smiles captured the essence of Likha: a true labor of love.
